About me

I am currently a 5th year Ph.D. candidate in the Institute of Computing Technology (ICT), Chinese Academy of Sciences, supervised by Prof. Qing He and co-supervised by Prof. Fuzhen Zhuang.

In 2017, I received my B.S. degree in soft engineering from Beijing Institute of Technology (BIT). During university, I was an International College Programming Contest (ICPC) participant and became a finalist in the fourth year.

From 2018 till now, I study as a research intern in Baidu Talent Intelligence Center (TIC) under the supervision of Dr. Hengshu Zhu. During this period of time, I'm also fortunate to be advised by Prof. Hui Xiong.

My general research interest includes data mining and machine learning, focusing on developing effective and explainable machine learning methods to reason from real-world talent, job, and labour market-related data. Here is my Google Scholar.
